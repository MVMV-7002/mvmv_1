The Supra MK1(1978-1981)

Introduction: The first-generation Supra debuted as a variant of the Toyota Celica, marking its shift into the luxury sportscar territory.

Engine: Initially equipped with a 2.0L straight-six engine, it was one of the few cars at the time to feature a six-cylinder engine in this category.

Key Features: The A40 had a more refined look than the Celica, with its longer wheelbase, more powerful engine, and improved suspension.
